About Me

I'm a 30-year-old Latina, 3rd generation Italian on my Father's side of the family, with a B.A. in Architecture and Urban Planning.
I moved from Brazil to Canada in 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic. It was a struggle to settle in with all the lockdown restrictions,
especially career-wise. With a background in set design for theatre and cinema, I looked for jobs in the cultural industry, which was
incredibly difficult since the cultural sector was one of the most affected during the pandemic.

With all the challenges of immigration and settling in professionally, I decided to pivot my career paths and look for new opportunities.
Since a very early age, I've shown interest in creative activities. I love drawing, painting, building, sewing, and everything that involves designing.
Looking for a career that allowed me to give life to my visions and ideas encouraged me to enroll in the Interactive Media Design program
at Algonquin College. I was compelled by the interdisciplinary approach of the program,
and I'm very excited about everything I've been learning.
